Beorn's Honey Cakes

Description

This recipe comes from the cookbook "Recipes from the World of Tolkien" written by Robert Tuesley Anderson. The cookbook is a compilation of recipes inspired by the Lord of the Rings and Hobbit movies and literature.

We will be making honey cakes, as served to the group by Beorn, the shapeshifter. It's no surprise his famous cake recipe calls for honey, as he is an ursine based shapeshifter. Join along as we transport our tastebuds into Middle-earth.

List of Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup, plus 2 tablespoons, unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 small, very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 4 tablespoons honey

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Line a 12-cup muffin pan with paper cups.
  3. Sift the flour, baking powder, and baking soda into a bowl.
  4. Mix together the melte butter, sugar, eggs, and mashed bananas in a separate bowl. Tip in the dry ingredients and mix together gently until evenly combined. Divide the cake mixture between the paper cups.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes or until risen and just firm to the touch.
  6. Transfer to a wire rack and drizzle each cake with 1 teaspoon of honey. Serve warm or cold.
